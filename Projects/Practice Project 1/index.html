Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and delicious omelette, ready in no time. This basic recipe provides a foundation you can adapt with your favorite fillings like cheese, vegetables, or herbs.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional: cheese, herbs, vegetables, cooked meats, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs until smooth and lightly seasoned with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan, allowing them to cook without stirring for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Gently fold or flip the omelette to finish cooking.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with optional fillings.
